Funding

This work is a part of the DIFUTURE project funded by the German Ministry of Education and Research (Bundesministerium für Bildung und Forschung, BMBF).
DIFUTURE is one of the four consortia in the Medical Informatics Initiative of Germany which aims at providing data of comprehensive depth and breadth to physicians and to medical researchers.

This work is part of the EKIPRO project which has received funding by the Intramural Resarch Funding of the Faculty of Medicine of the University of Augsburg.
EKIPRO studies the clinical gain of explainable artificial intelligence for clinical decision support using the example of prostate carcinoma in pathology.
